About Me

I am Zahra Zyanka Efendi, a UI/UX designer and web developer who has been actively building digital products since 2023. With a background in Information Systems, I focus on designing interfaces that are not only visually refined but also strategically structured to improve usability, efficiency, and user engagement.

I approach every project by understanding user needs and business goals, then translating them into clean, functional, and scalable digital solutions. My goal is to help organizations deliver better digital experiences through thoughtful design and well-executed implementation.

I design user-centered interfaces that simplify complex workflows and improve how users interact with digital products. From user flow mapping and wireframing to high-fidelity UI and prototyping, I ensure every design decision supports clarity, usability, and conversion.
